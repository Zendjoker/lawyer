Georgia Adoption Process

Understanding the steps involved in Georgia adoption proceedings

Initial Consultation: Discuss your adoption goals and legal requirements.

Petition Filing: File adoption petition with appropriate Georgia court.

Home Study: Complete required home study evaluation (when applicable).

Consent/Relinquishment: Obtain necessary consents from birth parents.

Background Checks: Complete criminal and social background investigations.

Court Hearing: Attend final adoption hearing before judge.

Final Decree: Receive final adoption decree and new birth certificate.
