Georgia Adoption Process

Understanding the steps involved in Georgia adoption proceedings

1

Initial Consultation

Discuss your adoption goals and legal requirements with our experienced attorney to determine the best path forward.

2

Petition Filing

File comprehensive adoption petition with the appropriate Georgia court along with all required documentation.

3

Home Study

Complete required home study evaluation when applicable to ensure a safe and stable environment.

4

Consent/Relinquishment

Obtain necessary consents from birth parents or terminate parental rights as required by law.

5

Background Checks

Complete all criminal and social background investigations required by Georgia law.

6

Court Hearing

Attend final adoption hearing before a judge who will review the case and issue the final decree.

7

Final Decree

Receive your final adoption decree and new birth certificate, officially completing your family's journey.
