Georgia Personal Injury Laws – Key Points

Important legal information for injury victims in Georgia

Statute of Limitations

Generally 2 years from the date of injury.

Comparative Negligence

You can still recover damages if you're less than 50% at fault.

No-Fault Insurance

Georgia is not a no-fault state—claims are made against the at-fault party.

Evidence Matters

Police reports, medical records, witness statements, and insurance communications strengthen your case.
