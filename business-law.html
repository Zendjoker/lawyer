FAQs (Quick)

Common questions about business litigation and contract disputes

Is a broken promise without a signed contract enforceable?

Possibly—promissory estoppel/unjust enrichment may apply.

Can we recover lost profits?

Yes, if reasonably certain and foreseeable (solid records help).

Do we have to sue?

Not always. Well-structured demands + ADR often resolve faster and cheaper.

Will a non-compete hold up?

Georgia enforces reasonable scope/duration per the Restrictive Covenants Act.
