Georgia Divorce Process

Understanding the steps involved in Georgia divorce proceedings

Filing Requirements: At least one spouse must be a Georgia resident for 6 months before filing.

Grounds for Divorce: Georgia allows both fault and no-fault divorce grounds.

Waiting Periods: 31-day waiting period after service of divorce papers.

Discovery Process: Exchange of financial information and documentation.

Settlement Negotiations: Attempting to resolve disputes outside of court.

Trial: Court proceedings if settlement cannot be reached.
